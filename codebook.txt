Filenames: smartphone_data.txt, smartphone_tidy_subdata

Variables
activity - list of 6 activities that subjects performed
subject - id for each participating subject
*mean & std indicate mean and standard deviation measurements
Time*Y - Time measurements for the duration of a force measurement in the given axis (X,Y, or Z)
Frequency*Y - Number of repetitions the given force measurement was recorded for the subject and activity.
